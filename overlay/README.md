## Intro

**Overlay** is a small Android app for a common issue: media apps often forget to implement background play and stop working when you lock the phone which means you lose audio the moment you turn the screen off.

Start playback, tap the overlay tile and dismiss quick settings. Overlay keeps a black layer on top so audio keeps going. This reduces  battery drain on OLED displays and also prevents accidental taps while your phone is in your pocket. When you are done press the power to remove the overlay.
