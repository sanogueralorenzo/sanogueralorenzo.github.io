The first movement was only air: curtain drag, a passing bus, glass tremor, the distant scrape of a chair from another apartment. The notation tried to make room for events too slight to perform on purpose.

Rain entered the margins before music did. A note beside the second staff instructed the player to wait until the room changes, which made every rehearsal dependent on weather and neighbors.

The score stopped when notation became too clean for the material. The window kept composing after the page gave up.

## Case Notes

The score is unfinished because it could not decide whether it was music or listening practice. Its most precise marks describe sounds no performer controls.

The unresolved question is whether composition can include the world without turning the world into a prop.
